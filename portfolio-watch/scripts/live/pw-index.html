The idea

The job isn't to report the market — it's to route your attention. Separate real signal from noise for a portfolio it has never seen, and only interrupt when it's worth it. Every threshold is relative to each holding's own volatility — that's what lets one rule set fit any portfolio. Full methodology & parameters: SKILL.md. Formulas are in the next tab.

① Information collection — six cross-checking sources

Signal comes from corroboration across independent lenses, not one feed:

· Price & volume — klines + market metrics (β, volatility, RVOL).
· Events & filings — earnings, guidance, dividends, 8-K, Form 4.
· Prediction markets (Polymarket) — live event probabilities (Fed cuts, elections) for catalyst theses & macro context.
· Smart money — insider & congress open-market buys/sells (10b5-1 excluded), cluster/CEO conviction.
· Options-implied — ATM IV → expected move, IV-vs-realized premium, put−call skew.
· Crypto microstructure — perp funding & open interest for crypto-linked names.
· Semiconductor cycle — TrendForce DXI memory index for semis.

Benchmark by asset class. Each holding is measured against the right ruler and endpoint: US stocks → SPY / sector ETF (OLS β, residual vol); crypto & crypto-linked → BTC (spot + perp funding); ETFs → their own daily bars. New names are auto-profiled; brand-new listings enter a cold-start path (sector-median prior + high-frequency bootstrap).

Crypto gate. Crypto & crypto-linked names are small-sample and high-variance (our backtest shows it), so they get a higher bar (×1.25) and must have volume confirmation to page — an unconfirmed crypto move is demoted one tier. A broken thesis is exempt (high-conviction): it still escalates straight to P0.

② Layered monitoring model

A · Price action — abnormal move (σ), gap, RVOL, 52-week levels, slow trends.
B · Events & filings — earnings/guidance, corporate actions, insider/activist filings, macro calendar (only if the book is sensitive).
C · Information & narrative — material news, aggregated estimate/rating changes, prediction-market event probabilities.
D · Portfolio — P&L vs the book's own vol, drawdown, concentration drift, correlation convergence, factor/exposure drift.
+ Context overlays — macro (rate/policy), sector (semi cycle), smart-money positioning — portfolio-level, never per-stock noise.

③ Three-check gate for a real move

A price move counts only after passing, in order:

1 · Statistically significant — in the holding's own adaptive σ (EWMA + robust MAD), threshold a t-quantile (small samples widen it automatically), not a fixed %.
2 · Idiosyncratic — significant after removing the market: z_idio = residual / residual-vol. A stock falling with the market isn't news; falling while the market rises is.
3 · Confirmed — abnormal volume or an identifiable cause (news / filing). A lone blip with no volume, no reason is watched, not shouted about.

Event/thesis signals (earnings, M&A, thesis break) are real by construction and skip the statistical gate.

④ Noise filtering

· β roll-up — a market-wide drop collapses ten correlated alerts into one portfolio line, not ten.
· Batch false-positive control — across N holdings × M dimensions, cap the false-discovery rate with Benjamini–Hochberg (q=0.10), not per-test α.
· Hysteresis ratchet — enter at 2.5σ, hold until 1.5σ; a reported move doesn't re-fire unless it escalates a tier.
· Continuation & cooldown — an already-reported move grinding on is silent; 4-hour same-dimension cooldown.
· Source-specific gates — 10b5-1 routine sales excluded; thin prediction/options markets gated on liquidity; low-liquidity pre-market ticks wait for the open.

⑤ Priority ranking when several fire

Score 0–100 = 0.30·severity + 0.25·portfolio-impact + 0.15·confidence + 0.10·novelty + 0.20·confluence − 0.40·noise. Ranked by impact on your money, not loudness — a 5σ move in a 1% position can matter less than a 2.5σ move in a 40% position.

Impact gate — a <2% position can't be P0 on price alone. Tiers — P0 push now / P1 digest / P2 interface. Merge — same ticker's many signals fuse into one card; same cause across tickers rolls up to one line. Budget — ≤4 P0 pushes/day (greedy by score); rather miss one medium signal than get muted.

⑥ Watch the thesis, not just the price

If you hold something for a reason — "MSTR as a leveraged BTC play", "ITB betting the Fed cuts" — the same residual math is re-pointed at that thesis's reference (BTC price, or a Polymarket event probability). When the reason breaks, that's the highest-order alert: it challenges your decision, not just reports a move, so it escalates straight to P0.
